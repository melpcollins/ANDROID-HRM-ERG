# Store Description

## App Title

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart

## Short Description

Heart-rate guided Zone 2 ERG training for indoor cycling

## Long Description

Build steady aerobic fitness with heart-rate guided Zone 2 training for indoor cycling.

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart connects to your Bluetooth heart-rate monitor and compatible smart trainer, then helps guide your ride using heart rate and ERG control. Instead of guessing whether you are riding too hard or too easy, the app helps you stay in the right aerobic training range for longer, steadier sessions.

This app is designed for riders who want a simple, focused indoor cycling experience built around heart-rate guided endurance work.

Use Zone 2 Cycling by Heart to:

- Follow heart-rate guided ERG workouts for steady indoor rides
- Stay closer to your intended Zone 2 effort
- Connect a Bluetooth heart-rate strap and smart trainer
- Monitor live workout feedback during your session
- Review workout summaries after the ride

Included workout modes:

- HR-ERG: adjusts trainer resistance in response to your heart rate
- Power-ERG: ride to a target power with a heart-rate ceiling
- Zone 2 Assessment: complete a guided assessment session to help judge aerobic durability

Why riders may like it:

- One click start
- Focused on Zone 2 indoor cycling rather than general fitness tracking
- Designed for simple setup and repeatable endurance sessions
- Supports heart-rate guided control instead of power-only workouts
- Built for riders training on an indoor smart trainer

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart is a practical training companion for endurance riders, time-crunched athletes, and anyone who wants more structure in steady indoor cycling sessions.

Important:

- Requires a compatible Bluetooth heart-rate monitor
- Requires a compatible Bluetooth smart trainer for ERG control features
- Not intended for medical use or diagnosis
