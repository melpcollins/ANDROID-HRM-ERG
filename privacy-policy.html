Privacy Policy

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart

Effective date: March 15, 2026

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart is operated by Mel Collins. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart may collect the following categories of information.

a. App and device information

The app may collect basic technical information such as:

  • app version
  • build number
  • Android version
  • device model
  • general app stability and crash information

b. Usage and analytics information

The app may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • app starts
  • workout type selected
  • workout started, paused, resumed, completed, or manually stopped
  • Bluetooth scan, connection, and reconnection success or failure
  • diagnostics export attempts

c. Bluetooth device information

The app can interact with Bluetooth heart-rate monitors and smart trainers. The app does not send raw Bluetooth device IDs or raw Bluetooth device names to backend analytics services. Saved Bluetooth device names and IDs may be stored locally on your device to support reconnecting and diagnostics export.

d. Diagnostics information

When you explicitly choose Export diagnostics, the app creates a diagnostics file on your device that may include:

  • saved Bluetooth device names and IDs
  • recent runtime events
  • recent workout session events
  • error messages useful for troubleshooting

This diagnostics file is only created and shared when you choose to export it.

e. Health and fitness-related information

The app processes heart-rate and workout-related data locally in order to run workouts and show workout feedback. The app does not send raw heart-rate samples or raw power samples to backend analytics services.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • operate the app
  • run Bluetooth setup and workout features
  • improve app reliability and performance
  • understand anonymous feature usage
  • diagnose crashes and technical problems
  • help investigate issues when you explicitly export diagnostics

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app may use third-party services including Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may process anonymous app usage, diagnostics, and crash-related information in accordance with their own privacy practices.

4. What Is Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • selected Bluetooth heart-rate monitor and trainer
  • workout settings
  • local diagnostics logs
  • exported diagnostics files that you choose to create

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share raw heart-rate samples, raw power samples, raw Bluetooth device IDs, or raw Bluetooth device names with backend analytics services.

If you choose to export diagnostics, you control where that file is shared, for example by email or another sharing option on your device.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics and crash information may be retained by the analytics providers used by the app.

Local diagnostics files remain on your device unless removed by the app or deleted by you. The app keeps a limited recent history of session diagnostics on-device.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Zone 2 Cycling by Heart is a fitness and training tool only. It is not a medical device and is not intended for medical use, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency monitoring.

9. Your Choices

  • You may choose whether to export diagnostics.
  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may remove the app from your device to stop future collection through the app.
