# Launcher Icon Prompt

Create a bold, minimal mobile app icon for an app called `Cycling HR ERG`.

Requirements:
- Main motif: a simple road bike combined with a heart
- The heart should feel integrated into the bike, not pasted on
- Clean geometric shapes, strong silhouette, readable at tiny launcher-icon sizes
- No text, no gradients that reduce legibility, no tiny details
- Style: modern, flat, athletic, friendly, slightly premium
- Background: solid teal or deep cyan circle on a light neutral field
- Accent: a vivid red heart
- Use high contrast and thick strokes
- Keep the composition centered and balanced inside a square canvas

Visual direction:
- White bike lines or shapes
- Red heart above or within the bike frame triangle
- Optimized for Android launcher visibility at small sizes

Output:
- Square 1024x1024 PNG
- Transparent-safe composition, but still works on solid backgrounds
